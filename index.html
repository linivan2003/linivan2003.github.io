What We Do

TutorMatch AI is an AI-powered platform that reimagines how students find academic support. Instead of sifting through endless tutor profiles and outdated search filters, students on TutorMatch experience an intuitive, swipe-based interface that matches them with the right tutor—fast, smart, and personalized. Built with both students and tutors in mind, TutorMatch AI focuses on connection over complexity. Our intelligent matching algorithm considers key factors like subjects, location, availability, learning preferences, and even age proximity to suggest highly compatible matches. With every swipe and interaction, the platform learns and improves—making each match smarter than the last.

AI-Powered Matching

Using machine learning and behavioral analytics, our algorithm dynamically profiles student learning styles, academic objectives, and engagement history to match them with the most compatible, high-performing math tutors in real time.

Expert Math Tutors

All our tutors are rigorously vetted and specialize in K-12 and college-level math topics.

Track Progress

Students and parents can view performance insights and track improvements through our intuitive dashboard.

Online & Flexible

Sessions are conducted online with flexible scheduling to fit any routine — no commuting, no hassle.

Interactive Learning

Our platform supports live whiteboarding, problem-solving, and instant feedback to maximize understanding.

Safe & Secure

We prioritize student safety with monitored sessions and secure, encrypted communication tools.
